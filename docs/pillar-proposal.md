# Pillar Proposal: Adrenalink Entities

A concise overview of the six core pillars/entities of the Adrenalink platform, each with a color identity and a focused value proposition.

---

## 1. Students
- Register, track, and manage student progress
- Gamified experience and achievement tracking
- Easy booking and transparent package selection

## 2. Teachers
- Automated commission and hours tracking
- Live schedule sync and lesson management
- Transparent communication with students and schools

## 3. Bookings
- Smart scheduling and rescheduling
- Real-time availability and conflict detection
- Centralized booking management for all stakeholders

## 4. Equipment
- Lifecycle management and usage tracking
- Damage, maintenance, and rental status monitoring
- Data-driven insights for equipment replacement

## 5. Payments (School Packages)
- Set and manage package pricing
- Transparent payment and commission breakdowns
- Real-time revenue analytics for schools

## 6. Rentals
- Equipment hire and rental management
- Track rental duration, status, and returns
- Integrated with booking and equipment lifecycle

---

Each pillar is visually represented by a unique color in the platform UI, reinforcing clarity and quick recognition for users.
