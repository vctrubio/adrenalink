# Pillar Proposal: Adrenalink Entities

---

## 1. Students [yellow]

- Browser home page,
- make a reservation
- synchronised lessons

## 2. Teachers [green]

- Manage and track working hours
- Commission-based salaries on every lesson
- Transparent communication with students and schools

## 3. Bookings [blue]

- Track progress
- Analysis & statistics
- Handle payments

## 4. Equipment [purple]

- Register your inventory
- Assign to specific teacher (or not)
- Track hours so you know when it's time to say goodbye

## 5. (School Packages) [orange]

- Import all your courses - lesson or rentals
- See which one is most popular
- Know where each reservation was made from

## 6. Rentals [red]

- Authorize students who are independent
- Manage equipment plannning
- Know who likes to break gear!
